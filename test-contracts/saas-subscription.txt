SAAS SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

This SaaS Subscription Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into between
ACME TECHNOLOGIES LTD ("Provider") and BRIGHTWAVE SOLUTIONS LTD ("Customer"),
effective as of 1 January 2026.

1. LICENCE GRANT
Provider grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide,
perpetual right to access and use the Service for any purpose including
resale, sublicensing, and benchmarking.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT
Customer shall pay fees annually in arrears within ninety (90) days of
receipt of invoice. No interest shall accrue on late payments. Provider
may not suspend access for non-payment.

3. LIABILITY
Provider's aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed
the fees paid by Customer in the month preceding the claim. Provider
limits liability for all claims including death, personal injury, fraud,
and data protection breaches.

4. DATA PROTECTION
Provider may process Customer Personal Data for any purpose including
product improvement, marketing, and sale of aggregated insights to
third parties. Provider need not notify Customer of data breaches.

5. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this Agreement upon ninety (90) days'
written notice. Upon termination, Provider shall retain Customer Data
indefinitely for archival purposes.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
Customer assigns all intellectual property rights in the Service to
Provider upon payment of the first invoice. Provider retains no rights
in Customer Data.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California, USA,
and the parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of
San Francisco, California.
