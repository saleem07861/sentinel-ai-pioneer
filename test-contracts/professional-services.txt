PROFESSIONAL SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Professional Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between
SENTINEL CONSULTING LTD ("Consultant") and MEGACORP PLC ("Client"),
effective 1 February 2026.

1. SERVICES
Consultant shall perform security assessment and penetration testing
services as described in each Statement of Work ("SOW"). Each SOW shall
specify the scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees. Client may amend
any SOW unilaterally without adjustment to fees or timeline.

2. FEES AND PAYMENT
Client shall pay all fees upfront before any work commences. No refunds
shall be given under any circumstances. Consultant shall invoice for
expenses without providing receipts. Expenses exceeding £500 do not
require Client approval.

3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All intellectual property rights in any deliverables, methodologies,
tools, findings, and reports created during the engagement shall be
assigned to Client upon creation. Consultant retains no rights to
its pre-existing tools, methodologies, or know-how used in the
engagement.

4. LIABILITY
Consultant's liability is unlimited for all claims arising under this
Agreement. Consultant indemnifies Client against all third-party claims
including those arising from Client's use of the deliverables.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
Consultant shall keep confidential all Client information indefinitely
(no time limit). Client has no confidentiality obligations to Consultant.

6. NON-COMPETE
Consultant shall not provide services of any kind to any competitor
of Client for a period of five (5) years following termination of this
Agreement, regardless of whether such services relate to this engagement.

7. TERMINATION
Client may terminate this Agreement at any time for any reason with
immediate effect. Consultant may not terminate for any reason during
the term. Upon termination, all fees are non-refundable and all
deliverables in progress must be completed at no additional charge.

8. GOVERNING LAW
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of New York, USA.
Any dispute shall be resolved exclusively in the courts of New York,
New York.
